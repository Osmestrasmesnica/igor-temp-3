About

Who we are

At Cybele Capital Partners, we empower our partners in natural resources and real estate to maximize value while driving the energy transition and upholding the highest ethical standards. Our expertise ensures sustainable growth and responsible investment in a rapidly evolving world.

  • Strategic advisory on mergers, acquisitions, and partnerships.
  • Optimisation of monetisation strategies for resource assets.
  • Dedicated focus on energy transition materials for a sustainable future.

Cybele Capital Partners offers unparallel global reach and local expertise. We are parnered with SJFMC Capital Limited, one of the leading investment advisor in India, their services include: Debt Syndication, Equity funding, Financial Advisor Services and Innovative funding solutions.

We are spanning London, UAE, Accra, Chennai, Canada, India, and Australia, we combine strategic expertise with deep market insights. Our focus extends beyond financial gains—we prioritise responsible investment in infrastructure and resources that drive sustainable growth.
